# AM Allegiance Clash RPG/MMO Player Guide

Welcome to the new Clash-inspired RPG/MMO system inside our Discord server.

This is a custom economy and progression game built around the same kind of loop that makes Clash fun: collect loot, upgrade your village, power up heroes, raid, help the clan, climb leaderboards, and take part in seasonal events.

This guide explains how to play, what each command does, and what systems are coming next.

---

## Quick Start

Start here if you are brand new:

1. Use `/daily` to collect your daily village loot.
2. Use `/farm` to grind Gold, Gems, and Clan XP.
3. Use `/train` to gain extra Clan XP.
4. Use `/upgradehall` to increase your Town Hall level.
5. Once unlocked, use `/raid`, `/openchest`, `/heroes`, and clan systems to progress faster.
6. Use `/village` to view your profile.
7. Use `/economyhelp`, `/phase3help`, and `/phase4help` to see command groups.

Your goal is simple: build your Discord village, upgrade your Town Hall, unlock heroes, earn loot, help the clan, and climb the leaderboards.

---

# Core Personal Economy

Your personal economy is your main account progression.

## Main Resources

You can earn and spend:

- **Gold** — main currency used for upgrades, chests, shop items, hero upgrades, donations, and raiding.
- **Gems** — rare bonus currency earned from raids, chests, bosses, and season rewards.
- **Raid Medals** — earned from raids, war systems, boss rewards, and season rewards.
- **Clan XP** — used for Town Hall progression and long-term growth.
- **Dark Elixir** — unlocks later at higher Town Hall levels.

## Important Commands

### `/daily`
Collect your daily loot from your village.

This gives:

- Gold
- chance at Gems
- Clan XP
- daily streak progress

Daily streaks give bonus Gold, so logging in consistently matters.

### `/farm`
Farm a dead base for safe resources.

This is your low-risk grind command. It gives Gold, possible Gems, and Clan XP.

### `/train`
Train your army for Clan XP.

Useful when you are trying to reach the next Town Hall upgrade requirement.

### `/raid`
Attack a base for higher-risk, higher-reward loot.

Raids can result in:

- failed attack
- 1-star raid
- 2-star raid
- 3-star raid

Better results give better rewards.

### `/openchest`
Spend Gold to open a War Chest.

Chests can give:

- Gold
- Gems
- Raid Medals
- Clan XP
- occasional bonus items

### `/village`
View your profile.

Shows:

- Town Hall level
- title
- Gold
- Gems
- Raid Medals
- Clan XP
- Dark Elixir, when unlocked
- daily streak
- raid record
- active boost charges
- achievements
- linked accounts

# Town Hall Progression

Your Town Hall level controls what you can do.

Use `/upgradehall` to upgrade your economy Town Hall.

You need both:

- enough Gold
- enough Clan XP

## Unlocks

- **TH1** — `/daily`, `/farm`, `/train`
- **TH3** — `/raid`
- **TH4** — `/raiduser`
- **TH5** — `/openchest`
- **TH7** — better chest odds and Legend Chest access
- **TH9** — Dark Elixir starts appearing
- **TH10+** — stronger hero and late-game progression

Town Hall progression is the backbone of the whole system.

# Shop, Items, and Boosts

Use `/shop` to view items.

Use `/buy item_key` to buy an item.

Use `/inventory` to see what you own.

Some items work with `/useitem`.

Phase 2 economy items work with `/useeconomyitem`.

## Useful Items

### Training Potion
Boosts your next few `/raid` rewards.

Use:

`/useeconomyitem training_potion`

### Resource Potion
Boosts your next few `/farm` runs.

Use:

`/useeconomyitem resource_potion`

### Builder Potion
Clears your farm, raid, and train cooldowns.

Use:

`/useeconomyitem builder_potion`

### Book of Heroes
Gives a large Clan XP boost.

Use:

`/useeconomyitem book_of_heroes`

### Rune of Gold
Gives a large Gold payout.

Use:

`/useeconomyitem rune_of_gold`

### Legend Chest
Premium chest with better reward odds.

Use:

`/useeconomyitem legend_chest`

### Loot Shield
Blocks the next steal attempt against you.

This works passively.

### War Banner
Temporary war/economy buff.

Use:

`/useitem war_banner`

# Clan Bank System

The clan has a shared economy too.

Use `/phase3help` to see clan economy commands.

## `/clanbank`
Shows the clan treasury, upgrades, and top donors.

## `/clandonate amount`
Donate your Gold into the shared clan bank.

Donating helps unlock clan upgrades and also gives you season XP.

Example:

`/clandonate 500`

## `/clanupgrade upgrade_key`
Leader/co-leader command that spends clan bank Gold on upgrades.

Current upgrades:

### Training Camp
Improves clan boss damage and future raid rewards.

### Clan Treasury
Improves clan bank prestige and future passive bonuses.

### War Academy
Improves boss rewards and future war economy scaling.

The more everyone contributes, the stronger the clan becomes.

---

# Clan Boss Raids

Clan bosses are shared PvE events.

Use `/phase3help` to see boss commands.

## `/startboss name hp`
Leader/co-leader command to start a boss.

Example:

`/startboss Goblin King 50000`

## `/boss`
View boss HP, time left, and top damage dealers.

## `/bossattack`
Attack the active boss.

You can attack once per cooldown period.

Damage contributes to the clan kill.

## `/claimbossrewards`
After the boss is defeated, participants can trigger rewards.

Rewards are based on damage contribution.

Boss rewards can include:

- Gold
- Gems
- Raid Medals
- Clan XP

# Seasonal Progression

The economy has monthly seasons.

Use:

- `/season`
- `/claimseason`
- `/seasonleaderboard`

## How to Earn Season XP

Currently, you earn season XP from:

- donating to the clan bank
- attacking clan bosses

Future systems may also give season XP.

## `/season`
Shows your current season level and rewards.

## `/claimseason`
Claims any unlocked season rewards.

## `/seasonleaderboard`
Shows the monthly season XP leaderboard.

Seasons reset monthly, so this gives everyone a fresh race.

# User Raiding PvP

Use `/phase4help` to see PvP commands.

## `/raiduser @member`
Raid another member’s village and try to steal Gold.

Your success chance depends on:

- your Town Hall level
- their Town Hall level
- your hero power
- their hero power
- active events

There is a cooldown to prevent spam.

## `/revenge`
If someone raids you, you may get a revenge opportunity.

Use `/revenge` to hit them back.

## `/bounty @member amount`
Place a Gold bounty on another member.

Example:

`/bounty @Player 1000`

PvP is meant to be fun chaos, not personal drama. Keep it playful.

# Clan Wars 2.0

Clan Wars 2.0 is a separate asynchronous war league-style system.

Use:

- `/startwar2`
- `/war2attack`
- `/war2status`
- `/endwar2`

## `/startwar2 opponent`
Leader/co-leader command to start a War 2.0 match.

Example:

`/startwar2 Enemy Clan`

## `/war2attack`
Use one of your War 2.0 attacks.

Each player has limited attacks per match.

Your result is affected by:

- Town Hall level
- hero power
- random attack outcome

Rewards can include:

- Gold
- Clan XP
- Raid Medals
- War Points

## `/war2status`
Shows the current War 2.0 score and top attackers.

## `/endwar2`
Leader/co-leader command to end the match and pay participant bonuses.

Boosts event-style gains and future reward systems.

# Future Upgrades Planned

The current system is only the foundation. More RPG/MMO features are planned over the coming weeks.

## Phase 5: Equipment & Rarity

Planned features:

- common gear
- rare gear
- epic gear
- legendary gear
- stat modifiers
- hero builds

This will let players customize heroes instead of simply leveling them.

Example future builds:

- high-damage Barbarian King
- defensive Grand Warden
- crit-focused Archer Queen
- PvP-focused Royal Champion

---

## Phase 5: Matchmaking

Planned features:

- MMR
- leagues
- Elo ladder

This will make PvP and competitive systems more fair and more meaningful.

Instead of random flexing, players will eventually be able to climb ranked ladders.

---

## Phase 5: Clan Territory Map

Planned features:

- region ownership
- seasonal conquest
- resource generation

The idea is to let the clan fight over territory, hold regions, and generate passive resources based on controlled areas.

This would turn the economy into a larger strategy game.

---

## Phase 5: Real-Time PvE

Planned features:

- cooperative raid bosses
- timed damage windows
- boss abilities and phases

Bosses will become more than simple HP bars.

Future bosses may have phases like:

- shield phase
- rage phase
- weak spot window
- final stand

This will make group events feel more alive.

---

## Phase 5: Marketplace

Planned features:

- player trading
- auction house
- black market trader

This would let players trade or sell items, creating a real player-driven economy.

The black market trader could rotate rare items or limited-time deals.

---

## Phase 5: AI-Generated Events

Planned features:

- random invasions
- seasonal story arcs
- dynamic economy fluctuations

This would make the game feel less predictable.

Examples:

- Goblins invade and steal from the clan bank unless defeated.
- Trader prices rise or fall for a weekend.
- A seasonal boss appears with unique rewards.
- Resource production changes based on event outcomes.
